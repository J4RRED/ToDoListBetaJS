To-Do List App

How To: Simply input items into the box below, hit "Click Me" and BAM! There's your To-Do List!

* Note: double-clicking your text will select all text so that you do not need to backspace every time.



Danger! Hitting 'enter' on your keyboard will cause the list to reset and all changes will be lost!

* Note: comment box can be resized by dragging the lower-right corner.
